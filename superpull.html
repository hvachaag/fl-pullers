Super Pull

Every February, FL Pullers hosts the premier Zellwood Super Pull — a two-night event drawing hundreds of trucks, tractors, and garden tractors from across North America to our world-class facility.

Arrival & Vehicles

All pullers are welcome to come early — we'll be on site before and after the event. Each puller is allowed two vehicles: your transport plus one (RVs included). Additional vehicles may park on the spectator side.

Camping

Dry camping is available on site for the weekend. Come early, set up camp, and enjoy the full Super Pull experience.

Registration & Driver Meeting

You must check in at registration each day during the posted registration times. A drivers meeting is held 30 minutes prior to pull start time each night.

Classes & Pull Times

We pull all classes both days. Check registration and pull times carefully so you don't miss your class. Details posted on Facebook closer to the event.
