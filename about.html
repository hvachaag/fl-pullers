Who We Are

The Heart of
Florida Pulling

A not-for-profit club carrying a tradition started over 50 years ago by five friends and a shared love of tractor pulling in Eustis, Florida.

The Florida Antique Tractor Club, Inc. was established in 1994, but our roots go back to 1971 when five men from Eustis, Florida organized the first-ever Sunshine State Tractor Pull — and truck pulling was born at the 1973 Kissimmee event shortly after.

We've grown from five founding members to more than 75 dedicated people. Our facility at the Zellwood Sweet Corn Festival grounds features a 400-foot, three-lane track widely regarded as the finest pulling track in all of Florida.

We proudly support Camp Challenge (Easter Seals Florida) and the Marion County Therapeutic Riding Association, and host an NTPA Grand National Series event each season.
