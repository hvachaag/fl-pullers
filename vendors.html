Food Vendors

Super Pull Food Vendor Details

Our Super Pull is a 2-day event drawing 2,500–4,000 spectators and approximately 300 pullers — a great opportunity for food vendors.

Booth Cost

$150/night

Per night for the 2-day event

Vendor Rules

  • Must provide your own power and lighting as needed
  • Water is available on site in the vendor area
  • Must be fully set up by noon on Friday (Day 1)
  • Take-down by end of Day 2 (we are on site before & after event dates)
  • You must keep your area clean at all times

Interested in a food vendor spot? Contact us or reach out on Facebook for availability and to reserve your space.
